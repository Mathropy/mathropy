Fill an $n \times n$ grid with numbers from $1$ to $n$, such that each number appears exactly $n$ times. The grid must satisfy the following conditions:
- Latin Square Property: No two identical numbers may appear in the same row or column.
- The Snake Path: There must exist a path (the "Snake") that:
  - Visits every one of the $n^2$ cells exactly once.
  - Moves only between orthogonally adjacent cells (up, down, left, right).
  - Starts at a cell labeled 1 and follows the sequence $1, 2, \dots, n, 1, 2, \dots, n, \dots$ until the end of the path.
